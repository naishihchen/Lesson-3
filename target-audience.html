Target Audience

The target audience for tihs website will mostly be parents with young children, or young children themselves. Other people will be able to use the website for weather forecasts, to be sure, but it's mainly a way for parents to interact with their children about the weather.

Persona 1

Mark Summers

Age: 32

Income: $80,000

Education: Bachelor's Degree

Marital Status: Single

Experience: Views weather on desktop site with children, usually for about up to an hour each time. Shows children the weather and explores some of the educational weather information.

Details of Accessing The Site: Mark accesses the site with his two kids after work, when he takes the to play outside at various parks and locations. When the weather is too bad for them to go outside, he will teach them about the weather and how to identify it.

Persona 2

Sarah Hale

Age: 38

Income: $60,000

Education: Bachelor's Degree

Marital Status: Married

Experience: Views weather on desktop site with children, usually no more than half an hour. Shows children the weather and views one or two of the educational pages with them

Details of Accessing The Site: Sarah is a mom of three with two kids in school and one not yet old enough for school. She takes the young one out to parks and brings all three out to outdoor local activities when possible. She'll check the weather before she goes and wants to involve the kids in the process, to teach them how to do it and to teach them about the weather.
