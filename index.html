Site Name

The name I have chosen for my site is Forecast Friend. I want it to be a site that feels friendly and easy to access, and can offer some educational information to parents and children about the weather.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site will be to provide a 1 week current weather forecast for users in their area, but it will also provide some helpful educational information for parents and children about different kinds of weather, how to stay safe and how to identify dangerous weather.

Site Domain
